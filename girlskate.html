ABOUT ME

GIRLS+ SKATE 613

I had the honour of creating shirts and a few illustrations for Girls+ Skate in Ottawa, a non-profit organization that helps young people of all identities empower themselves through skateboarding. They meet up every week and teach anyone who comes by how to skateboard.

The client loved two of the illustrations almost equally, so we decided to use both. One was used for shirts and they were both used for posters and promotional material.

CAMPUS PRIDE WEEK

In 2017, I got to create a logo and look for Pride Week at Algonquin College. This is one of my all-time favourite projects I've completed to date; I was able to come up with a look that was fun, cool, and completely different from the rest of the Pride Centre. The theme of the week was Blast Off, which made creating posters and icons easy as they were all space-themed.

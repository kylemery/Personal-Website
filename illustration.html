ABOUT ME

ILLUSTRATION

Illustration is something I've been doing my entire life. Only recently have I been able to apply my skills to my job, which stretches my abilities and helps me grow as an artist. I love to learn new mediums and push my limits.

A lot of my illustrations are based off of fantasy concepts and medieval imagery. I adore classical paintings, especially the work by John WIlliam Waterhouse. I love going to art galleries and art shows, and I love supporting local art.

ALGONQUIN PRIDE CENTRE

The Algonquin Pride Centre was created in 2016 at Algonquin College and was created to be a safe, open, inclusive space for all students no matter their sexual orientation or gender identity.

I was very fortunate to have been able to come up with all the branding for the centre while adhering to the Students' Association guidelines.

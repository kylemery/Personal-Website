ABOUT ME

SHARP AS KNIVES

Sharp as Knives is an artisanal Ottawa-based label specializing in screen printed apparel other other illustrated items. My partner and I created this side-project in 2017 and we hand make all the items we sell. Each item is selected and made with the utmost care, and every design has love poured into it.

We started this company in May 2017 and haven't looked back. I love screenprinting and working hard creating these products, and I love selling them online and at craft shows. The most amazing thing about doing something like this is that we get to meet new people, learn new skills, and come home happy.
